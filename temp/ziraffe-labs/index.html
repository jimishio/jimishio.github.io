ParkEasy: Real Time Parking Assistance
Zirrafe Labs
Motto
  • Don’t spend precious time driving around looking for parking.
  • Don’t pay more for parking, than you have to.
  • Figure out if you’re likely to find parking before you plan your trip.
  • Receive real time updates on parking availability near your destination.
How it will work
By simply talking to your phone.
1) ParkEasy: I need the closest parking now.
2) ParkEasy: I need the cheapest parking now.
3) ParkEasy: I need parking near HA6 1BJ now.
4) ParkEasy: I need parking near NE16 6NS in 90 minutes.
How will ParkEasy respond
1) The closest parking is at 7 Hudson Street, a 7 minute walk from your current location. Would you like directions?
2) There is no parking available within 15 minutes walking distance of HA6 1BJ now. Nearest available parking is within 22 minutes at 2 pounds per hour. Would you like directions?
Other features
Map of Real Time Parking Availability/Cost near a Postcode.
Future
Reserve/Pay for Parking, without stepping out of your home, office or car.
